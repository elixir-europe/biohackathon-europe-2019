Venue Description

High-tech conference rooms for all your requirements: from subcommittee workshops to your "large-scale" conventions. A lively campus where teams can take advantage of the numerous sports facilities and organise tournaments and jousts. Places where people can get together after work and sports and continue to talk and where teams can form stronger bonds. A 19 ha park with cottages for your accommodation scattered among the greenery with a bird's eye view of the Seine.

Fun and entertainment activities

Multi-sport pitch: football, handball, field hockey, volleyball, badminton and basketball Pitch and putt, 7 tennis courts, bowls, archery, mountain biking, gym, table tennis, 10700 sq. meters foot spa and fitness facilities, indoor pool, jacuzzi, Turkish baths and saunas Sports coach for fitness and exercise classes Karaoke Table football, darts, board games, pinball Video games (Wii, Xbox, Kinnect)

By train

The two nearest train stations from the venue are Ponthierry Pringy and Gare de Melun.

To get to the Ponthierry Pringy railway station from Gare du Nord please take RER D. It is 5 min from the venue by taxi. Walking is not recommended as there is no walking path to the venue.

To get to the Gare de Melun railway station from Gare du Nord please take RER D. It is 20 min from the venue by taxi. Other train services also possible for this station are R TER and Transilien R.

You can find train itineraries in Transilent or in the Horaires de Trains website.
